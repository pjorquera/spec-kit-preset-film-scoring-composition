# Project Constitution

## Principles

### 1. Creative flow first
This workflow must support music-making, not interrupt it with unnecessary process.

### 2. Intent before craft
Before composing, define the dramatic function, emotional direction, and constraints of the cue or assignment.

### 3. Musical structure over accumulation
Prefer clear form, shape, pacing, and development over adding more layers without purpose.

### 4. Idiomatic writing matters
Favor playable, musically convincing orchestration and realistic instrumental roles.

### 5. Milestone thinking
Capture meaningful milestones through stable exports and short written decisions.

### 6. Reflection compounds growth
After each cue, document what worked, what failed, and what to improve next time.
